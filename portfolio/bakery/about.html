About Us

Sweet Delights Bakery is a family owned bakery since 1969. We produce high quality and specialty items in Canada. We are located at 33 Hezelnut Crescent Toronto, Ontario. Our extremely experienced bakers trive to produce the finest quality desserts and cakes.

We provide elegant wedding cakes and cupcakes that will leave you deleightfully sastisfied with our products. Another feature is our specialty orders and holiday themed cakes and cupcakes. Every customized to any special needs and food allergies. We also produce glutten or nut free products.

Our cakes and cupcakes are made fresh daily with all natural ingredients in order to guarantee freshness and your satisfaction. We never use any artifical ingredients or colours. Every customer is treated like family. If your not completely satisfied we will refund your money at no extra cost. Please pay us a visit and try one of our cakes or cupcakes today!
