Here's a time line of David Bowie's life:

1947

Born David Robert Jones - Brixton, South London.

1959

First instrument for Christmas (Saxophone).

1962

Punched in the eye by George Underwood during a fight for a girl.

1969

Bowie Releases Space Oddity.

1970

Releases Man Who Sold The World.

1971

Appears on the cover of Hunky Dory.

1972

Adopts persona Ziggy Stardust. Releases The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders From Mars.

1973

Bowie releases Aladdin Sane.

1974

Releases Diamond Dogs

1975

Young Americans is released. He creates the Thin White Duke persona.

1983

Bowie releases Let's Dance. Bowie's best selling album

1984

Bowie gets his first Grammy.

1987

Bowie releases Never Let Me Down

1996

- Inducted into Rock & Roll hall of fame.

2000

David Bowie's Daughter Alexandria Zahra Jones is born.

2006

Honoured with grammy lifetime achievement award.

2013

On his 66th birthday, Bowie announces the release of The Next Day, his first studio album in a decade.

2016

Releases his final studio album Blackstar on his 69th Birthday.

2016

Dies at the age of 69 after battling cancer.
